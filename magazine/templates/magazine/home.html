Welcome to ISFDB Explorer

If you want to see a list of all the stories Robert Heinlein published in The Magazine of Fantasy and Science Fiction during his career, or if you’re interested in the names of every book published by Poul Anderson (and when they were published), then you have come to the right place.

This web site uses data from the excellent Internet Speculative Fiction web site (at isfdb.org). Our queries are done on a backup copy of the isfdb database that was downloaded in early February 2026, so any additions and changes made to the isfdb database since then are not included in our reports.

As of March 2026 there are four features implemented, with more to come:

  • Enter an author’s name (or part of their name) in order to display basic biographical data, together with links to lists of their magazine and book publications.
  • Enter a magazine’s name (or part of the name) together with a year or year and month, in order to view a detailed list of an issue’s contents.
  • Enter a magazine’s name (or part of the name) together with an author’s name (or part of the name) in order to view a list of all stories that author published in that magazine, with links to the contents of the individual issues.
  • View a list of all 92 magazines in the database, with the ability to click through to view the contents of any issue.
